## Concept
1. Tank battle is an open-world head-to-head tank combat game.
2. The terrain will be used for tactical advantage.
3. The focus will be on flow and feel.

## Rules
1. You can move anywhere in the terrain, which is surrounded by mountains
2. Both players start with finite health and ammo
3. Each hit takes away health
4. Last player standing wins

## Requirements
### SFX
Gun firing, explosion, barrel moving, turret moving, engine sound.

### Static mesh
Simple tank comprising tracks, body, turret and barrel.

### Textures
Later-on we'll want to add textures for visual flare.

### Music
Background music to create tension.
